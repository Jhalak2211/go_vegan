Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of your favorite vegan bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, or microgreens

Method:

  1. Toast the slices of vegan bread to your liking.
  2. Cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado. Mash the ingredients together with a fork until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  5. Optional: Top with sliced cherry tomatoes, a sprinkle of red pepper flakes, or a handful of microgreens for added flavor and nutrition.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vegan avocado toast!
